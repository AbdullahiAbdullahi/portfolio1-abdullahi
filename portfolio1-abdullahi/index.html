About Me

I'm a student at Cambridge Rindge and Latin School with a passion for using technology and game design to create meaningful educational experiences. As a member of Innovators for Purpose, I've had the opportunity to develop games that help students explore career paths and discover their potential.

My work focuses on combining narrative-driven storytelling with interactive gameplay to create engaging learning experiences. I believe that games can reshape education, promote equity, and help young people explore their identities in powerful ways.

Beyond game design, I'm committed to supporting my community through tutoring and mentoring, helping students from under-resourced backgrounds achieve their academic goals. I'm always eager to learn new skills and take on challenges that push me to grow as a developer and creative problem-solver.
